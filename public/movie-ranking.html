Malani's Best Movies

Top three of all time - according to Malani.

Moulin Rouge

This film moves me to tears no matter how often I watch. The emotional layering in the music is uncontested.

La La Land

I've heard people say nothing happens in this movie - to which I have to respond, "I guess you might be right, if you ate cement as a baby." What a gorgeous film with wit, color, emotional complexity, sacrifice... an ode to the arts.

Bridesmaids

Hilarious every time. And I love films that center around women's companionship.
